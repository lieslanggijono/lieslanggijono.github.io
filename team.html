The Innovators Behind Helio

Achieving global health is an interdisciplinary effort. Helio was founded by two curious and determined teens, working to tackle global health issues with emerging technologies. Our objective is to tackle the data problem, since before solving any problem, it’s important to know where the problem exists and to what degree.

I’m interested in bridging gaps in healthcare that are often overlooked yet can cause tremendous negative implications. Whether it be certain data gaps that lead to the inaction of seemingly invisible conditions like vitamin deficiency, or gaps caused by oppression so that equality in research & development can be achieved.
